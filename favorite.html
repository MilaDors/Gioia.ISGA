BACK

The Public Library of Amsterdam on the
Oosterdok is ideal for students.

The library is at the central of Amsterdam
Which means close to public transportation
which makes is accessible for everyone.

A lot of students come here to study
for their midterm or just for their homework.
Because it is a library there are a lot of free
books you can use inside the building.

If you get motivated by other students
working, this is the place for you.

Entering the building is free which
is ideal for students who are short on money.

There is a restaurant if you do want
something to eat or drink.

There is a lot of space for you to sit.

Between 12pm and 3 pm, it is the most crowded.
There is also free Wi-Fi.
